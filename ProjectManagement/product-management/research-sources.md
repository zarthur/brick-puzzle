## Research Observations To Preserve

- Competitors show demand for aim-and-shoot brick-breaking with many levels, powerups, offline play, and leaderboards.
- Player complaints frequently mention forced ads, unskippable ads, unclear reward systems, difficult levels tuned around boosts, and powerups/currencies that feel monetized.
- The product opportunity is a fair, puzzle-forward alternative with free powerups and optional support-only monetization.
- Apple guidance makes copycat risk and App Review clarity important from the beginning.
- Product page assets should be treated as testable launch hypotheses through Product Page Optimization once traffic exists.
